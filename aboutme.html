ABOUT LUCY

I started baking with my grandmother teaching me. She had a lot of old recipes that were written down; some that she made and some that were passed down in her family. I really loved how much we got to spend time together when baking so I always wanted to bake with her. Eventually, I got old enough and wanted to try it more myself, and I don't get to bake as much with my grandmother anymore. But I have grown to love baking on my own because I get to make all the decisions and I have learned to do some recipes from the heart and not measure anything out.
